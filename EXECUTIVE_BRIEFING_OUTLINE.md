## Section 2: Transaction Governance (8 minutes)

### What Is a System of Record?

- Single source of truth for transaction state
- Immutable event log (what happened, when, by whom)
- Closing readiness computed from facts, not opinions
- Audit narratives generated deterministically

### How It Works (Without Technical Details)

1. **Every action is an event** (contract signed, appraisal ordered, title cleared)
2. **Readiness is computed** (green = ready, yellow = conditional, red = blocked)
3. **System explains itself** ("This deal is blocked because...")
4. **Audit trail is automatic** (hand to regulator, done)

### What This Means for You

- You know the truth about every deal, every day
- Failed closings become preventable, not inevitable
- Regulatory inquiries become routine, not crises
- Authority is enforced, not assumed
